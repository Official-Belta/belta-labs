2.1 What is Impermanent Loss

Impermanent Loss (IL) is the value loss compared to simply holding (HODL) assets when providing liquidity to an AMM. It is called 'impermanent' because the loss disappears if prices return to the original level, but in practice prices rarely revert, making it effectively a permanent loss.

3.5 3-Layer Hedging System — Underwriter Pool Profitability Structure

BELTA IL Hedge Protocol은 단순 보험Pool 모델의 구조적 Limitation(UW 수익 불안정)를 해결하기 위해 3가지 레이어를 결합하여 모든 Phase에서 Underwriter가 수익을 낼 수 있는 구조를 설계했다.

4.1 Premium Structure — Voluntary LP Contribution

LPs pay a premium from a portion of their fee income and receive partial IL compensation. Since premiums come from earned fees rather than principal, psychological acceptance is higher than TVL-based payments.

Premium = 12% of LP fee income (Approximately 2.7%/year in TVL terms)
Coverage = 35% of IL (partial hedge — ensures insurance pool stability)
LP Net Benefit (2020~2025 backtest): Even paying 12% of fees, IL compensation (35%) is larger. Without hedge +4.0%/yr → with hedge +7.8%/yr. LPs earn an additional +3.7%p annually even after costs. IL concentrates in black swan epochs, and 35% coverage compensation exceeds the cumulative 12% premium cost.

LP Entry Barrier = 0: Simply provide liquidity to a V4 Hook pool for automatic hedging. No separate enrollment, monitoring, or rebalancing needed. Completely eliminates operational complexity compared to direct Perps hedging — passive risk infrastructure.

6.1 V3 IL Correction Formula

IL is amplified vs V2 in Uniswap V3 concentrated liquidity range [Pa, Pb].

IL_V2(r) = 2·√r / (1 + r) − 1 where r = P_exit / P_entry IL_V3 = IL_V2 × 1 / (1 − √(Pa/Pb))

6.2 온Chain IL 계산 — Epoch 기반 누적 Method

단순 진입/청산 가격 비교는 가격이 범위 밖으로 나갔다 돌아오는 경우 IL을 과소 측정한다. BELTA는 epoch 기반 누적 Method을 사용하여 Medium 경로의 IL을 정확히 반영한다.

// afterAddLiquidity: Position Snapshot position.entryPrice = currentSqrtPriceX96 position.sqrtPa = TickMath.getSqrtRatioAtTick(tickLower) position.sqrtPb = TickMath.getSqrtRatioAtTick(tickUpper) position.liquidity = liquidityDelta position.epochStart = block.timestamp // afterRemoveLiquidity: IL Calculation and Settlement hodl_value = token0_entry × exitPrice + token1_entry actual_value = token0_exit × exitPrice + token1_exit il_amount = max(hodl_value − actual_value, 0) payout = il_amount × COVERAGE_RATIO // 0.35

② V4 Hook Native — 거버넌스 변경 제로. 이 프로토콜은 Uniswap V4 Hook만으로 구현되며, 코어 프로토콜 수정이 전혀 필요 없다. V4 Hook 시스템의 가장 실용적인 DeFi 보험 유즈Case이며, V4 생태계 확장이라는 Foundation의 핵심 미션과 직접 부합한다.
